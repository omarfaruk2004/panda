Facts

  • Panda's distinct black-and-white markings have two functions: camouflage and communication.
  • Panda bears have vertical slits for pupils.
  • Mother pandas keep contact with their cub nearly 100% of the time during their first month.
  • Giant pandas are good swimmers and excellent tree climbers.
  • They have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food.
